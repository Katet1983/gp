Toy-Match is a matching-3 game. You can be relaxed in this game. The mission is to collect some directional things. It is easy, just match three or more same color blocks to a line. If you match the blocks with the background, it will collect them. When you collect all the props, you can continue the next level. But your moves are limited, you should better collect them before running out of moves. Sounds difficult? Do not be upset, we will mention you if you actually do not know how to move. Let us enjoy the game!
