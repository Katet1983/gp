A daily traffic jam awaits you to clear it. Your goal is to make the police car slide through the exit by moving other cars. Move the cars and trucks up and down, left and right, until the road is cleared so the police car can get out of the exit. There are four difficulties here and you can choose to add or not add walls to make the game more difficult. There is also a time timer to see how long you can help the police car get out of the exit.
