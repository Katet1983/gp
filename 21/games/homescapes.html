ABOUT Homescapes

Description

Austin, the butler, returns to his childhood home full of memories, but what awaits him is the decision of his parents to sell the house. Players need to help him complete the task of renovating his childhood home to prevent this big house full of childhood memories from being sold. During this period, players need to help Austin solve various renovation problems encountered in the house, gradually into Austin's family life, to experience the warmth of family life!

Game features.

● Unique gameplay: swap and match items to help Austin renovate the house!

●Interior design: You can decide the appearance of the house.

● Exciting triple elimination levels: full of fun, with unique boosters and explosive combinations!

● A huge and beautiful mansion: Discover all the secrets it holds!

● Wonderful characters: Watch their lives and interact with each other in the in-game social network.

● Cute pets: Meet a mischievous and fluffy cat.

●Invite your Facebook friends to help you create your own cozy atmosphere in the house!

How to play

A combination of puzzle elimination + management, players collect stars through elimination to obtain resources, constantly unlock the home area, and decorate according to preference; the game has beautiful graphics, the plot is deep, the game is easy to play and fun!

The core gameplay is based on swapping two adjacent elements to form rows or columns of at least three elements. Players can obtain useful power-ups and activate them by double-clicking on them or switching other elements.
